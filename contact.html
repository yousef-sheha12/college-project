Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Before reaching out, check if your question is answered below. These are the most common questions we receive from our customers every day.

Q. How long does delivery actually take?

Our average delivery time is 28 minutes from the moment you place your order. This can vary slightly based on your distance from our kitchen, time of day, and current order volume. You can track your order in real time through our app or website. During peak hours (Friday and Saturday evenings), deliveries may take up to 40 minutes — we always communicate any delays proactively.

Q. What if my order is wrong or something is missing?

We're human, and very occasionally mistakes happen. If your order is incorrect or an item is missing, please contact us within 30 minutes of delivery via the app, by phone, or by WhatsApp. We will either send the missing item immediately (if within range) or issue a full refund for the affected items — no questions asked. We take quality control very seriously and every reported issue is reviewed by our kitchen manager.

Q. Do you cater for dietary restrictions and allergies?

Absolutely. Our menu clearly labels all items that are vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, dairy-free, or halal-certified. We can also accommodate many custom requests — just add a note to your order. For serious allergies (nuts, shellfish, etc.), please call us directly so our kitchen team can take extra precautions. We cannot guarantee a 100% allergen-free environment, but we take all allergy requests extremely seriously.

Q. What are your delivery hours and coverage area?

We deliver 7 days a week, from 10:00 AM to 11:30 PM. We currently serve Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, and parts of The Bronx. Enter your address on the order page to confirm coverage in your specific neighborhood. We're actively expanding — if we don't cover your area yet, join our waitlist and we'll notify you when we do.

Q. Can I schedule a delivery in advance?

Yes! Our scheduled delivery feature lets you place an order up to 72 hours in advance and choose a precise 15-minute delivery window. This is perfect for meal prepping, planning office lunches, or ensuring dinner is ready exactly when you need it. Scheduled orders receive priority in our kitchen queue and are never delayed.
