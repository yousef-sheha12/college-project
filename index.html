Cooking With Heart Since 2015

FeastHouse started as a tiny kitchen in Brooklyn with two chefs, one dream, and an obsession with flavor. Today, we're a family of over 80 passionate food professionals serving thousands of happy customers every single day. Our mission has never changed: to make extraordinary food accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Journey 🗺️

Every great restaurant has a story. Ours began with a broken oven, a borrowed van, and an unshakeable belief that people deserve truly great food delivered to their homes. Here's how we got from there to here.

15

2015 — The First Kitchen

Co-founders Marco and Lena launched FeastHouse from a 400 sq ft commercial kitchen in Brooklyn, New York. With just a menu of 8 items and a secondhand delivery van, they made 12 deliveries on their first day. Every single customer left a 5-star review. The dream was real.

17

2017 — Expansion & First Restaurant

After two years of explosive growth, FeastHouse opened its first physical restaurant on Smith Street. The wait list for tables was over 3 months long. We also hired our first 5 additional chefs and expanded our delivery zone to cover all 5 boroughs of New York City.

20

2020 — Going Digital

When the world changed, we adapted fast. We launched our digital ordering platform, introduced contactless delivery, and expanded our menu to 90+ items. Despite the challenges, 2020 was our highest-growth year yet — serving comfort food to people who needed it most.

24

2024 — National Recognition

FeastHouse was named "Best Food Delivery Service" by Food & Wine Magazine and featured in the New York Times. We expanded to 6 cities, launched our subscription meal plan, and hit our milestone of 1 million deliveries made. We're just getting started.

Our Core Values 💎

Great food is built on strong values. These are the principles that guide every decision we make — from which farms we source from to how we train our delivery drivers.

🌿

Sustainability First

We partner exclusively with local farms that use sustainable practices. Our packaging is 100% compostable, and we offset our entire carbon footprint through verified reforestation programs. Good food shouldn't cost the planet.

🤝

Community & Inclusion

We actively hire from underserved communities, offer paid culinary apprenticeships, and donate 2% of every order to local food banks. FeastHouse believes that access to great food is a right, not a privilege.

🔬

Quality Without Compromise

Every ingredient goes through a rigorous quality check before it enters our kitchen. We reject up to 30% of deliveries from suppliers that don't meet our strict freshness and ethical sourcing standards. No exceptions.

❤️

Customer Obsession

We read every single review. We call back every complaint. We celebrate every compliment. Our customer satisfaction score has been above 96% for three consecutive years, and we're not stopping until it hits 100%.

Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Before reaching out, check if your question is answered below. These are the most common questions we receive from our customers every day.

Q. How long does delivery actually take?

Our average delivery time is 28 minutes from the moment you place your order. This can vary slightly based on your distance from our kitchen, time of day, and current order volume. You can track your order in real time through our app or website. During peak hours (Friday and Saturday evenings), deliveries may take up to 40 minutes — we always communicate any delays proactively.

Q. What if my order is wrong or something is missing?

We're human, and very occasionally mistakes happen. If your order is incorrect or an item is missing, please contact us within 30 minutes of delivery via the app, by phone, or by WhatsApp. We will either send the missing item immediately (if within range) or issue a full refund for the affected items — no questions asked. We take quality control very seriously and every reported issue is reviewed by our kitchen manager.

Q. Do you cater for dietary restrictions and allergies?

Absolutely. Our menu clearly labels all items that are vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, dairy-free, or halal-certified. We can also accommodate many custom requests — just add a note to your order. For serious allergies (nuts, shellfish, etc.), please call us directly so our kitchen team can take extra precautions. We cannot guarantee a 100% allergen-free environment, but we take all allergy requests extremely seriously.

Q. What are your delivery hours and coverage area?

We deliver 7 days a week, from 10:00 AM to 11:30 PM. We currently serve Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, and parts of The Bronx. Enter your address on the order page to confirm coverage in your specific neighborhood. We're actively expanding — if we don't cover your area yet, join our waitlist and we'll notify you when we do.

Q. Can I schedule a delivery in advance?

Yes! Our scheduled delivery feature lets you place an order up to 72 hours in advance and choose a precise 15-minute delivery window. This is perfect for meal prepping, planning office lunches, or ensuring dinner is ready exactly when you need it. Scheduled orders receive priority in our kitchen queue and are never delayed.
