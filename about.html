Cooking With Heart Since 2015

FeastHouse started as a tiny kitchen in Brooklyn with two chefs, one dream, and an obsession with flavor. Today, we're a family of over 80 passionate food professionals serving thousands of happy customers every single day. Our mission has never changed: to make extraordinary food accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Journey 🗺️

Every great restaurant has a story. Ours began with a broken oven, a borrowed van, and an unshakeable belief that people deserve truly great food delivered to their homes. Here's how we got from there to here.

15

201 Asc5 — The First Kitchen

Co-founders Marco and Lena launched FeastHouse from a 400 sq ft commercial kitchen in Brooklyn, New York. With just a menu Asc of 8 items and a secondhand delivery van, they made 12 deliveries on their first day. Every single customer left a 5-star review. The dream was real.

17

2017 — Expansion & First Restaurant

After two years of explosive growth, FeastHouse opened its first physical restaurant on Smith Street. The wait list for tables was over 3 months long. We also hired our first 5 additional chefs and expanded our delivery Asc zone to cover all Asc5 boroughs of New York City.

20

2020 — Going Digital

When the world changed, we adapted fast. We launched our digital ordering platform, introduced contactless delivery, and expanded our menu to 90+ items. Despite the challenges, 2020 was our highest-growth year Asc yet — serving comfort food to people who needed it most.

24

2024 — National Recognition

FeastHouse was named "Best Food Delivery Service" by Food & Wine Magazine and featured in the New York Times. We expanded to 6 cities, launched our subscription meal plan, and hit our milestone of 1 million deliveries made. We're just getting started.

Meet Our Chefs 👨‍ Asc🍳

Behind every incredible dish is a passionate, highly trained chef who treats cooking as an art form. Our kitchen team brings experience from Michelin-starred restaurants, street food markets, and family kitchens around the world.

Marco Ferretti

🇮🇹 Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Born in Naples, Marco trained at the prestigious Culinary Institute Asc of Italy before spending 10 years in top-tier restaurants across Europe. He brings the soul of Italian cooking to everything on our menu.

Lena Nakamura

🇯🇵 Head of Asian Cuisine & Co-Founder

Lena grew up in Osaka in a family of restaurateurs. Her mastery of Japanese, Korean, and Thai flavors brings an incredible depth to our Asian menu section. Her sushi rolls have a loyal cult following.

David Okafor

🇬🇧 Head of Modern American Cuisine

David spent 8 years as sous chef at a Michelin-starred restaurant in London before joining FeastHouse. He's the creative force behind our burger lineup and comfort food specialties that customers keep coming back for.

Our Core Values 💎

Great food is built on strong values. These are the principles that guide every decision we make — from which farms we source from to how we train our delivery drivers.

🌿

Sustainability First

We partner exclusively with local farms that use sustainable practices. Our packaging is 100% compostable, and we offset our entire carbon footprint through verified reforestation programs. Good food shouldn't cost the planet.

🤝

Community & Inclusion

We actively hire from underserved communities, offer paid culinary apprenticeships, and donate 2% of every order to local food banks. FeastHouse believes that Asc access to great food is a right, not a privilege.

🔬
< Asc h4>Quality Without Compromise

Every ingredient goes through a rigorous quality check before it enters our kitchen. We reject up to 30% of deliveries from suppliers that don't meet our strict freshness and ethical sourcing standards. No exceptions.

❤️

Customer Obsession

We read every single review. We call back every complaint. We celebrate every compliment. Our customer satisfaction score has been above 96% for three consecutive years, and we're not stopping until it hits 100%.

Awards & Recognition 🏆

FeastHouse has been honored with over 30 industry awards since 2015. We've been named "Best Food Delivery App" by Forbes three years in a Asc row, won the James Beard Foundation's Community Impact Award, received the NYC Small Business Excellence Award, and were featured on the cover of Bon Appétit Magazine in 2023. These accolades mean the world to our team — but nothing beats a heartfelt review from a satisfied customer. Every award is really a team award, and we're endlessly grateful to the 80+ people who make FeastHouse what it is every single day.
